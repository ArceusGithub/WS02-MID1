Akagami Shanks

About Shanks

Shanks Red-Haired Shanks is a legendary and powerful pirate in the Grand Line, being the captain of the Red Haired Pirate Crew and one of the Four Emperors in the second half of the grand line.

Shanks Characteristics

  1. Captain of the Red Hair Pirates, and an Emperor of the Sea.
  2. Shanks has a special Haki ability where he can prevent others from seeing into the future.
  3. Shanks is the only member of the original Four Emperors to not be a member of the Rocks Pirates.
